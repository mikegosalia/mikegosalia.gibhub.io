Mike Gosalia

Novelist

Mike Gosalia was born and raised in the suburbs of Kansas City. He spent his youth like most kids do, playing sports (cross country and tennis), studying, making art (sketches and photography), and observing the world around him. He loved to read books and was charmed by them at an early age. He attended college at The University of Chicago where he studied English language & literature and pre-med. After college, he decided to travel the world, visiting places like Paris, France and Mumbai, India. At one point, he began to do a masters in philosophy in Paris. That didn’t work out, but it had been a great learning experience for him.

In his early thirties, Mike moved to Seattle, where he currently lives. Here he began to take his imagination seriously, expressing and sharing what he likes best: novels. When not writing these, Mike likes to read, watch the occasional indie film, and play sports. He is currently doing his Master of Fine Arts in Creative Writing at Pacific Lutheran University, where he will graduate in 2022.
