Mike Gosalia

Author & Novelist

Mike Gosalia was born and raised in the suburbs of Kansas City, Kansas, where he spent his youth studying, playing sports, and watching action films. He attended college at The University of Chicago where he studied English Language and Literature. He spent his twenties traveling overseas, living for a time in India and in France.

In his early thirties Mike moved to Seattle, where he currently lives. Here he began to take his imagination seriously, deciding to express and share what he likes best: stories. When not writing, Mike jogs, collects vinyl records, plays tennis, kickboxes, and spends time with friends and family.
